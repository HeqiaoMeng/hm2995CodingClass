Introduction

Pipa is a kind of traditional Chinese instrument, dating back to about 2,000 years ago, Qin Dynasty. It is honoured as the ‘ king of Chinese national music’. There are more than 60 kinds of techniques of playing Pipa. It can simulate lots of sounds in the nature, such as the sound of wind and hooves. The tone of Pipa can be fierce and strong, so it is good at expressing the sound of battle, such as ‘ The Ambush on All Sides’. In addition, Pipa can be played leisurely and gently, such as ‘ A Night of Flowers and Moonlights by the Spring River’.
